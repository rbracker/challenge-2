About Me

My name is Ranee Bracker, and I am a proud member of the Navajo Nation. At 25 years old,

I am a dedicated professional with a strong background in retail management.

Currently serving as an Assistant Store Manager at Dunn-Edwards Paints, I have

accumulated over four years of experience, starting as a Sales Associate and progressing to my current role.

While my journey in the paint industry has been rewarding, the desire for a better work-life

balance has prompted me to explore new career opportunities. As a single parent to my two-year-old daughter,

Mika Smiley, finding a fulfilling career that allows me to be present for her is paramount.

My extensive research into remote work led me to discover the field of Software Engineering.

The prospect of problem-solving and finding innovative solutions in this domain resonated with me.

I am confident that my experience in addressing challenges and driving solutions in my current role

has equipped me with a valuable skill set for a career in software engineering.

Upon completion of a rigorous boot camp, my aspiration is to contribute my skills

and dedication to a dynamic tech company such as Microsoft or Amazon. I am particularly drawn

to the vibrant tech community in Seattle, and relocating to the area aligns with my long-term goals.

I am enthusiastic about the prospect of transitioning into software engineering,

recognizing it as an opportunity not only for personal growth but also to create a better

future for myself and my daughter. I am committed to leveraging my problem-solving

abilities and work ethic to excel in this exciting field.
